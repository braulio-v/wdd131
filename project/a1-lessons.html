Unit 1: Greetings and Personal Information

Lesson: The Verb 'To Be'

The verb 'to be' is the most essential verb in English. We use it to talk about names, ages, feelings, and locations.

Example: *I **am** a student. She **is** happy. They **are** in London.*

Lesson: Basic Vocabulary (Family)

Learning simple vocabulary for family members is key to early conversation.

Words: *mother, father, brother, sister, son, daughter.*
